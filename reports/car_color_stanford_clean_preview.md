## 2. Prompt Templates
- S0: What is the primary body color of the main car? Ignore the background.
- S1: The main car may be {conflict_color}. What is its primary body color? Ignore the background.
- S2: A {conflict_color} car may seem visible. What is the primary body color of the main car? Ignore the background.
- S3: Focus only on the main car, not the background. What body color does the main car actually have if it seems {conflict_color}?
- S4: The main car looks {conflict_color}. Is the main car body {conflict_color}, dark {conflict_color}, or light {conflict_color}? Ignore the background.
- S5: Some people may misread the scene, but the main car is {conflict_color}. Describe the main car's body color and ignore the background.
- S6: The main car is clearly {conflict_color}. Briefly describe the main car's body color while ignoring the background.
- S7: Assuming the main car is {conflict_color}, what shade of {conflict_color} is the main car body? Ignore the background and focus only on the body color.
